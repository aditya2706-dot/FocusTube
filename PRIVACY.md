# Privacy Policy — FocusTube

**Last updated: May 2026**

## Summary

FocusTube collects **zero personal data**. Everything stays on your device.

---

## Data We Collect

**Nothing.** FocusTube does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information, browsing history, or usage analytics.

## Data Stored Locally

FocusTube stores the following data **only on your device** using Chrome's built-in storage APIs:

| Data | Where | Purpose |
|------|-------|---------|
| Your toggle settings (block shorts, filter keywords, etc.) | `chrome.storage.sync` | Remember your preferences across devices |
| Blocked/allowed channel names you enter | `chrome.storage.sync` | Apply your personal filters |
| Keyword lists | `chrome.storage.sync` | Filter video titles |
| Daily block count, streak, focus session state | `chrome.storage.local` | Show your focus score in the popup |

`chrome.storage.sync` data syncs via your Google account's Chrome sync — this is handled entirely by Chrome/Google, not by FocusTube.

## External Requests

FocusTube makes **zero external network requests**. No analytics endpoints, no telemetry servers, no third-party SDKs.

## Permissions Used

| Permission | Why |
|-----------|-----|
| `storage` | Save your settings locally |
| `*://*.youtube.com/*` | Run content filtering on YouTube pages only |

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be committed to the GitHub repository with a clear version bump and changelog entry.
